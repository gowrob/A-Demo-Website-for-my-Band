Molat Band

Created by GOWROB

Since 2017

Formed in 2017, Molat is a rock band of BAUET. "Isolation-er Chithi" is the 1st original track of Molat Band.,
It's about the ongoing Corona pandemic and how people are dealing with this worst situation, awaiting for the good days that are not very far.

Music arrangement and composition: Molat
• Lyrics: Razeen
• Tune: Shuvasis Sarkar Gowrob (Keys)
• Mixing & Mastering: Taawkir Tajammul (Studio Chanachur)
Script and Concept: Molat
• Direction: Razeen
• D.O.P: Shovon Rahaman, Amit Abrito
• Editing & Colour: Redwan Zoy
